Our Mission



At Chocotonic we believe in giving back to the rainforests, a source that without, we would not be able to exist. To help with the preservation and sustainability of the rainforests, we donate a portion of all proceeds gained through our chocolate.



Currently we're donating 35% of all proceeds to various charities that work to preserve the rainforests, and we're always looking for more ways to give back.
